# Skill Swap Website - Project Idea

## Objective
A skill exchange platform for students where they can swap skills, take exams, earn certificates, and view their rankings on a leaderboard.

## Key Features
- **Skill Exchange**: Students can list skills they have and skills they want to learn.
- **AI Skill Matching**: Matches students based on their skills and rankings.
- **Exam System**: Students can take tests to verify their skills.
- **Leaderboard**: Displays top students based on performance.
- **Certificates**: Automatically generated certificates upon successful exam completion.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Full control over users, skills, exams, and results.

## Technical Requirements
- **Backend**: PHP
- **Database**: MySQL (SQL)
- **Authentication**: Secure login/auth.
- **Admin Credentials**:
    - Email: `skill@admin.com`
    - Password: `skill@access.com` (to be hashed in DB)
- **UI/UX**: Premium, modern, and high-end design.
